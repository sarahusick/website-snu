I work in a variety of materials, themes and contexts. I enjoy juxstaposing unlikely mediums and visual languages in paintings and installations. I have founded the artist-led project Loft Art Gallery, a pseudo artist residence in rural Manitoba. I complete heterogeneous work series under fictional artist names. Some subjects that interest me are rural Canadian landscapes, "high" art history fusions with contemporary packaging design, idealized imagery place through the lens of tourism marketing, the grey area between print and digital media, and AI and meaning generation. And most of all, I hate artist statements.
